About

Twitter Status allows users to check out latest 20 twitter status based on a screen name (link to explain) with queried user's location, user since, date of last update, total status updates since beginning of time, frequency of total status updates, frequency of recent status updates, number of followers, number of friends, and followers to friend to ratio.
Users can also visualize how post counts correlate with like and Retweet counts.
When a data point on graph is clicked, the top Tweet that contributed to most likes and retweets of the month will be returned. The tweets container will scroll to corresponding Tweet automatically.
